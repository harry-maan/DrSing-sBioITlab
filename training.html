Training Programs

The Dr Singh's BioIT lab offers two Internship programs and various training programs. Internship programs being conducted as per institute's norms, whereas training programs can be conducted on request.

Internships

6-Month Program (Dissertation): This structured training is designed for final-semester postgraduate students (M.Sc., MPH, M.Tech) in fields like Public Health, Epidemiology, and Biotechnology, requiring a one-time fee of ₹10,000.

Short-Term Program (2–3 Months): Aimed at motivated graduate and postgraduate students for hands-on research exposure, this program focuses on areas like Bioinformatics and Health Social Sciences and typically involves a fee of ₹5,000.

Key Requirements: Both programs require an application endorsed by the student's Head of Institution, a 500-word Statement of Purpose (SOP), and a minimum of 80% attendance for certification
